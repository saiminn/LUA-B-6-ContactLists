Level Up Academy's Alumini Network

Vision

We envision being a proactive association dedicated to addressing the community's needs and fostering sustainable positive change. Our vision is to actively contribute to the development and well-being of the community.

Mission

To fulfill our vision, our mission is to support the community's needs in education, livelihood, health, social services, and emergency response. Additionally, we aim to empower junior students to actively engage in the Association's actions, fostering a sense of responsibility and community service.

Goals

To play a pivotal role in shaping the 'State Federalism' process, actively participating in its establishment, building, and formation.

Objectives

  1. Coordinate with the academy to provide the basic needs of current students.
  2. Facilitate the sharing of educational opportunities, work opportunities, personal development, and professional development among members.
  3. Implement various fundraising activities to generate funds for alumni and community support
  4. Establish and operate an Alumni fundraising business to ensure a regular income for sustained support activities.
  5. Represent the academy in various forums and events to enhance its visibility and reputation.
  6. Maintain regular communication with the academy and current students to assess their needs and provide support accordingly.
  7. Regularly update a contact book of alumni members to facilitate effective communication and networking.
  8. Organize regular meetings and conferences to ensure the sustainability of the association and empower members to realize their dreams.
  9. Conduct a minimum of three fundraising activities per year to support the alumni fund and associated activities.
  10. Implement a fundraising business with at least one alumnus in charge, sharing profits with the in-charge and the broader alumni network.
  11. Advocate all members to actively cooperate in implementing the above objectives for collective success.
  12. Actively seek fundraising opportunities to supplement the association's financial resources for expanded impact.
By aligning our efforts with these vision, mission, goals, and objectives, we aim to create a lasting positive impact on both our alumni community and the broader society.
